Hello, world.

Mhm, this is the sixth version of my webpage. Every version that I’ve built up
until now, I’ve neglected to update it with new posts after publishing because,
well, they’re *annoying* to update; I end up trying to make these pages *smartly*
with shiny new frameworks, gimmicks, and other tricks that would “make it easier
for me to write this time around, surely!”

I’ve also realized that in my pursuit of “smart solutions,” I just end up tying
myself down to more SaaS products. I think for the past half-a-decade now, my
website has been exclusively hosted on Vercel? If I’m going to make good on my
promise to slowly move towards self-hosting the tools I use on a day-to-day basis,
I might as well start with my personal website.

So, here we are today with a static website built with Eleventy and hosting on a
Hetzner cloud server. Nothing fancy, just markup languages and CSS. I already have
a good feeling about this website with how lighter it feels. *Let’s just hope that
I can actually stick to consistently writing now, for my own sake.*

Welcome to the brand new homepage of Marco Tan(gents).
